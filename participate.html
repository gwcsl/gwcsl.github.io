Participate- ElfCon

ElfCon is about participation! Wearing only an elf hat is lame. Bring friends. Wouldn’t it be more fun if you brought your whole family of elves? If you decorated your sleigh? Be creative! It’s all about having fun. And drinking hot cocoa, of course.
Follow ElfCon on all its social media (@officialelfcon on Twitter and Instagram) to know the route and be alerted of all ElfCon news. Post photos of your costume and your favorite hot cocoa moments! Help spread the ElfCon word-- retweet, forward, like, make an ElfCon TikTok Dance!
Want to have the MOST fun? Donate to ElfCon’s charity drive below to get your ELF PASSPORT and ELF APPROVED stamp. The completion of this passport will give you access to a special ElfCon prize!
